## 🧩 What is MindOS?

MindOS is a tool that helps you work together with artificial intelligence. It creates a shared space where your digital agents, context, and knowledge work as one. This system syncs your thoughts and skills globally. You control how your data moves and evolves with the AI agents. MindOS aims to give you a transparent and adaptable digital mind.

---

## 📋 Key Features

- **Agent Collaboration**: Manage multiple AI agents that understand you.
- **Unified Memory**: Store and recall information easily across devices.
- **Skill Building**: Let agents learn and improve skills alongside you.
- **Context Awareness**: Keep your data organized by context automatically.
- **Global Sync**: Access your mind-space from anywhere with an internet connection.
- **Control & Transparency**: See and adjust agent actions anytime.

## 💾 Step 2: Install MindOS

1. After downloading the `.exe` file, find it in your **Downloads** folder.
2. Double-click the file to start the setup.
3. If Windows shows a security message, click **Run** or **Yes**.
4. Follow the installation prompts:
    - Choose an install location or use the default.
    - Click **Next** on each screen.
    - Wait while MindOS installs.
5. When setup finishes, click **Finish**.

---

## ⚙️ Step 3: Launch MindOS

- Find the MindOS icon on your desktop or in the Start menu.
- Double-click to open the app.
- The first time you launch it, you might need to allow access to your network for syncing data.

---

## 🔑 Step 4: Create Your MindOS Account

To keep your thoughts and agents synced, you need an account.

1. Open MindOS and click **Sign Up**.
2. Enter your email and create a password.
3. Check your email inbox for a confirmation message.
4. Click the link in the email to verify your account.
5. Return to MindOS and sign in with your new credentials.

---

## 💡 Step 5: Start Using MindOS

- Create your first AI agent by clicking **New Agent**.
- Give your agent a name and description.
- Add notes, skills, or tasks for the agent to assist with.
- MindOS will store and organize this data automatically.
- Sync your data to the cloud by clicking the **Sync Now** button.

---

## 🌐 Syncing and Using Multiple Devices

Once you have an account, MindOS keeps your mind connected globally.

- Install MindOS on any Windows device.
- Sign in with your account.
- Your agents, context, and knowledge-base update automatically.
- Use this feature to keep your digital mind consistent everywhere.

---

## 🔧 Customizing MindOS

You can adjust settings to fit your workflow:

- Set sync frequency (manual or automatic).
- Pick which agents share data.
- Manage storage size and clear cache.
- Enable or disable notifications.

---

## 📂 Backup and Restore

MindOS keeps your data safe and easy to restore.

- The system backs up your mind data regularly to the cloud.
- To create a manual backup, go to **Settings > Backup**.
- To restore, choose **Restore Backup** and pick the latest file.

## 🛠️ Troubleshooting Common Issues

- **App won’t start**: Restart your computer and try again.
- **Sync fails**: Make sure you are connected to the internet.
- **Login problems**: Reset your password via the **Forgot Password** link.
- **Installation hangs**: Check disk space and close other apps.

## 🧑‍💻 How MindOS Works

MindOS uses AI agents that act like helpers in your mind. They keep track of your thoughts, store knowledge, and learn over time. These agents support various tasks by using context-aware memory and skills. The system’s transparency lets you see what agents do and lets you control their actions. This way, your digital mind evolves with you.

---

## 🔐 Privacy and Security

Your data is secure. MindOS uses encryption when syncing your mind with the cloud. Only you can access your account and data. You control what data agents see and store. MindOS does not share your information without permission.
